Cereal

Description

A deliciously simple bowl of cereal. Nothing more, nothing less.

Ingredients

  • A bowl
  • A spoon
  • 250ml of Milk
  • Your preferred cereal

Optional

  • 1 Banana
  • A knife

Steps

  1. First, and always first, grab your cereal of choice and pour however much you want on the bowl.
  2. Next, and always after you have poured the cereal, pour the milk.
  3. Optionally, slice a banana into 1-inch coins and throw them (not vigorously) in the bowl
  4. Grab your spoon and enjoy!
