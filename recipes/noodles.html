Cup Noodles ++

Description

These really are just cup noodles with an egg in them

Ingredients

  • A Bowl
  • A Pack of Instant Noodles
  • A Stove/Burner/something you can cook with
  • A Small Sauce Pan
  • An egg
  • Filtered Water (don't be nasty bro come on)

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove.
  2. Now, pour water into your bowl. Protip:You want to pour a half inch more than the amount of liquid you want in the final product.
  3. Next, pour the water you measured out in the sauce pan and place the sauce pan in the stove.
  4. Once the water is boiling, open the pack of instant noodles and place the block of dry noodles in the boiling water.
  5. Then, once the noodles are no longer a block, pour in whatever flavourings came in the pack of noodles and mix them to form a broth.
  6. Finally, crack an egg and place it on the pan. Then cover with a lid. Check on it to make sure you don't overcook the yolk (its the best part, trust).
  7. Now pour into the bowl and enjoy!
